FAQ

Is this keyboard quiet?

Yes. The gasket mount and internal dampening reduce noise significantly, especially when paired with silent switches.

Does it require soldering?

No. All switches are hot-swappable.

Can I remap keys?

Yes. VIA and QMK support full remapping and macros.
